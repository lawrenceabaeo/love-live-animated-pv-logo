Pure CSS3 Animated Logo copying Love Live School idol project

What is this?

This pure css3 animated logo mini-project copies the animated logo seen in promotional videos for the Japanese entertainment property ‘Love Live School idol project’. An example of one of those videos can be viewed here: Click here for example video

What are its Features?

When opening the webpage for this project, an animated logo executes in the style of the noted videos.

Why did I make this?

I wanted to see how far I could get in copying the noted video’s opening logo animation using only CSS3 animations. I was able to get about 90% - I could not copy the particle effects that happen when the English letters turn to Japanese. In that case, I substituted an animation that uses the existing letters to get a ‘feel’ for what occurred in the original.

What technology skills were learned/retrieved?

CSS3 animations

What are this project’s limitations?

  • The animation does NOT scale and is NOT mobile responsive. Use the browser’s zoom function to achieve additional zooming.
  • The project is NOT compatible with older browsers.
  • There is no high resolution background.
  • The fonts are approximate because I do not want to pay to use the exact commercial fonts.

Tech references

  • Koruri font (for the japanese words)
  • Saxmono font(for the word ‘School idol project’)
